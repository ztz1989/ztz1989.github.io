Tianzhu Zhang

23 Avenue d'Italie, 75013 Paris

I was born in China, 1989. In 2012, I graduated from the computer science department of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) with Bachelor of Science degree. In October 2014, I obtained my Master of Science degree of computer and communication networks engineering from Politecnico di Torino in Turin, Italy. From November 2014 to November 2017, I was a PhD candidate in a joint program between Politecnico di Torino and Telecom Italia, under the supervision of Prof. Paolo Giaccone and Dr. Pino Castrogiovanni. From June to August 2017, I was involved in a 3-month internship in Nokia Bell Labs (Paris), under the supervision of Dr. Massimo Gallo. From October 2017 to November 2019, I held a PostDoc position at Laboratory of Information, Networking and Communication Sciences (LINCS), in Paris, France, under a grant from Telecom ParisTech and Cisco Systems. I had the pleasure to collaborate with Prof. Dario Rossi, Prof. Luigi Iannone, Dr. Leonardo Linguaglossa, and Prof. James Roberts. My research focused on Software Defined Networking (SDN), Network Function Virtualization (NFV), and High-speed packet processing. Currently, I am also highly interested in Machine/Deep Learning, Network Security, and Internet of Things (IoT). Please feel free to drop me if you have any suggestions or topics.
